Bail Funds

The following organizations are collecting donations on behalf of individuals currently held to bail, including protesters who have recently been arrested. The list has been broken down according to state; national organizations are listed at the top.


  • National

    • The Bail Project

      A registered non-profit actively bailing out protestors in select cities in addition to their overall efforts

    • Split Bail Fund Donation

      This fund splits contributions between more than 40 bail funds, mutual aid funds, and racial justice organizers

    • The Freedom Fund

      A fund focused on, but not exclusive to LGBTQ+ individuals

    • National Bail Out

      A Black-led and Black-centered organization, National Bailout coordinates the annual #BlackMamasBailOut in addition to their year-round efforts

  • Alabama

    • The Montgomery Bail Out

      Focused on helping inmates in the wake of COVID-19, has bailed out several proestors as well

  • Arizona

    • Tucson Second Chance Community Bail Fund

      This fund provides aid for defendants across Pima County, AZ

    • Black People's Justice Fund

      Focused on freeing Black folx and LGBTQIA+ people

  • California

    • Peoples City Council Freedom Fund

      A Los Angeles-based fund organized to cover bail, court fees, medical costs, and supplies for protestors

    • Bay Area Anti-Repression Committee Bail Fund

      A community-organized fund working hard to provide legal support for protestors in the Bay Area

    • Silicon Valley Democratic Socialists of America

      This organization currently has a temporary bail fund as well as a fund for COVID-19 aid projects

    • DeDe McClure Memorial Community Fund

      A community fund working to free and support imprisoned organizers and activists

  • Colorado

    • Colorado Freedom Fund

      A bailout organization based in Denver that provides support in the Denver, Arapahoe, Jefferson, Adams, Douglas, El Paso, and Boulder Counties

    • Funds for Protesters Against Police Brutality

      A fundraiser focused specifically on bailing out individuals arrested while protesting

  • Connecticut

    • Connecticut Bail Fund

      This organization runs several support programs, including a Women's Group, an Immigrant Bail Fund, and a Family Justice Hub

  • Delaware

    • FNB Bail Fund

      A fund offering support for folks jailed in the Justice for George Floyd protest in Wilmington, DE

  • Florida

    • Florida Justice Center

      This organization works to support individuals before and after incarceration

    • Community Bail Fund

      Community Bail Fund has offered to match donations up to $250,000

    • Alachua County COVID-19 Bond Fund

      This fund is specifically focused on freeing community members to mitigate the spread of COVID-19

    • Jax Community Action Support Fund

      Donations to this fund will go to support local protesters

    • (F)empower Community Bail Fund

      This fund is organized by (F)empower, a queer, abolitionist group based out of Miami, FL

  • Georgia

    • Atlanta Solidarity Fund

      This organization works to bail out activists who have been arrested for participating in social justice movements

    • Forsyth County Community Bail Fund

      This fund is currently focusing on protesters who have recently been arrested, and on defendants who are particularly vulnerable to COVID-19

  • Hawaii

    • Hawaii Community Bail Fund

      This fund is a project of the Democratic Socialists of Honolulu and allies

  • Illinois

    • An organization dedicated to helping Muslims in pretrial incarceration

      This fund is organized by the Black Lives Matter Bloomington chapter, and will be used to help individuals arrested while doing advocacy and activism work

  • Iowa

  • Louisiana

  • Massachusetts
